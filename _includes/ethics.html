--- --- IT Ethics Guidelines
    In today's technological landscape, ethical considerations in IT play a crucial role make sure our data stays private, our technology is used properly and that we use it responsibly. These guidelines also help in making fair AI, protecting ideas, and stopping bad incidents like cyberbullying. IT ethics help us make smart choices with technology, aiming for a better society while thinking about what's right and wrong.

    1/ Developer Guidelines

    - Informed Consent: Ensure users' informed consent when collecting data or implementing functionalities.
    - Security Measures: Prioritize robust security measures to safeguard user data against breaches and unauthorized access.

    2/ Privacy and Security

    - Privacy: Incorporate privacy considerations into the development of systems and applications from the outset.
    - Consent and User Rights: Protect user's data and respect user rights regarding consent, data access, and deletion, complying with relevant privacy laws and regulations.

    3/ Cyberesecurity Ethics

    - Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing: Employees should follow ethical standards and legal regulations while conducting security assessments and penetration testin according to the EC-Council - Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH).
    - Cyber Practices: Promote responsible user behavior and adherence to cybersecurity best practices to prevent cyber threats according to the National Cyber Security Centre - Cyber Essentials.

    Reference and Citation

    2. National Cyber Security Centre - Cyber Essentials
